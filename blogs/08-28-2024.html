Start of the School Year

08-28-2024

So it's been some time since I updated this. I've been pretty busy figuring out what I want to do with myself in terms of a career in programming. I'd really like to see if I can rebuild this website in React, but at the same time I've been exploring backend projects, which I always thought was what I wanted to do for a job.

I've also been pretty active on LeetCode. I used to avoid any kind of algorithmic problem solving like the plague, but somehow I've come a point where hitting the 'submit code' button and seeing the golden words "all testcases passed" on the first try is a dopamine rush unlike anything else.

It's also worth noting that the school year just started. One problem I had with many of the CS classes I took at Virginia tech was that the courses I was required to take seemed really out of touch with what companies want you to know nowadays, even for internship positions.

In fact, one of the classes I'm in this semester will have us build a full stack application from the group up, as our semester project. Such a class isn't available to undergrads until possibly their senior-level capstone class, and here I am taking it in grad school.

I'm not sure how useful my other classes will be, but I'm sure I'll still learn a lot from them. Only time will tell, though.
